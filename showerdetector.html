How to Use

  1. Click "Start Listening" and allow microphone access
  2. Phase 1 (10s): Play real shower noise
  3. Phase 2 (10s): Play non-shower sounds (speech, music, bathroom fan)
  4. Watch the RMS (upper plot) and Flatness (lower plot) curves
  5. Green "ðŸš¿ ON" tag appears when the detector identifies shower sounds
  6. After both phases, the system will suggest new thresholds
  7. Click the suggestion to apply and continue testing
  8. Iterate until false-positives are gone and true-positives remain
